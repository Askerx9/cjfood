History and positioning

CJ Fruits is a family business, importer, wholesaler, based in Brussels. It is by listening to market trends and sanitary regulations that we have become a true multi-specialist fruit, in perpetual evolution. We take special care in the selection of our producers, each specialist in their field. This allows us to offer a wide range that covers almost all permanent fruits, seasonal or off-season in conventional

Our vision

Nutrition is a key factor in being healthy. Eating 5 fruits and vegetables a day remains a major public health issue. That's what drives the CJ Fruits team every day.

Our convictions & ambitions

The fruit is a food for all generations, always trend, which combines accessibility, simplicity, pleasure, form and well-being.

  • Accessibility
    there is a wide range of fruits less expensive than a pastry, a pastry, an appetite suppressant.
  • Simplicity / practicality
    there is always a fruit that you can take away, wherever you are, which requires no preparation or cover to be tasted.
  • Pleasure
    the variety of fruits can satisfy all tastes. From the appetite suppressant to the tasting basket, alone or as an accompaniment, it is always a pleasure to consume or to offer.
  • Fitness and well-being
    to be healthy, it's as simple as a fruit.
